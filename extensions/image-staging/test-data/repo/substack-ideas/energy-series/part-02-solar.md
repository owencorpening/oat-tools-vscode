## The Sun's Energy

The Sun provides an enormous amount of energy to Earth every day. Solar power is one of the most abundant renewable energy sources available.

## How Solar Panels Work

Solar photovoltaic (PV) panels convert sunlight directly into electricity through the photovoltaic effect.

## Benefits

- Renewable and abundant
- Low maintenance
- Increasingly cost-effective
- Can be deployed at scale
